How the service works

  1. Client contacts Techno Web Masr to request a business account.
  2. A dedicated tenant workspace is provisioned for that business.
  3. The client connects only assets they own or manage (Facebook Pages / WhatsApp Business assets).
  4. The client uses the CRM to receive and reply to customer communications.

How we use Meta Platform Data

Techno Web Masr uses Meta Platform Data only to provide the requested business messaging and CRM functionality. Clients connect their own assets, and each client can access only its own Pages, WhatsApp accounts, conversations and related data. We do not sell Platform Data.

This page describes the product relationship for clients and reviewers. It does not claim Meta partnership or endorsement.

Security and privacy

  • Access tokens are stored encrypted and are not displayed publicly.
  • Each business works in an isolated tenant workspace.
  • Access to operational tools is controlled through authenticated panels.
  • OAuth exchanges and webhook processing run through secure server-side flows.
  • Public privacy and deletion instructions are published on the application domain.
